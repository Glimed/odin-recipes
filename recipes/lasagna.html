Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food that layers rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and tender pasta sheets, all baked together to perfection. This hearty dish is topped with a golden layer of melted mozzarella and parmesan, making it a warm, savory, and satisfying meal. Whether served for a family dinner or a special occasion, lasagna is the ultimate crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • LAsagna Noodles (9-12 sheers, cooked or no-boil)
  • Ground Beef (1 lb, browned)
  • Italian Sausage (1/2 lb, optional, crumbled)
  • Tomato Sauce (3 cups)
  • Crushed Tomatoes (2 cups)
  • Tomato Paste (2 tbsp)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Onion (1 medium, chopped)
  • Ricotta Cheese (2 cups)
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (3 cups)
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese (1 cup)
  • Egg (1, for the ricotta mixture)
  • Fresh Basil (optional, chopped, for garnish)
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)
