Pizza

A Meat Lovers Pizza is a hearty, savory pizza piled high with a variety of meats. It typically includes classic options like pepperoni, sausage, bacon, and ham, all on a delicious pizza crust, topped with a generous layer of melted cheese and marinara sauce. The combination of rich, savory flavors makes it a favorite for meat enthusiasts!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni (Sliced)
  • Italian Sausage (Crumbled and cooked)
  • Bacon (Cooked and crumbled)
  • Ham (Sliced)
  • Ground Beef (Cooked and seasoned)
  • Olive Oil (Brushing the crust)
  • Died Oregano (Optional garnish)
